💡 Quick Guide & Best Practices

  • Save/Load Projects: Save your work regularly as JSON files. Use "Load Project" to restore.
  • Multi-Part Projects: Maximum 15 parts per project. Click "Add New Part" at the bottom.
  • Multiple Setups: Each part can have unlimited operations (OP10, OP20, etc.).
  • Auto-Calculation: All fields calculate in real-time with validation.
  • Safety Factors: 1.2x (Proven) | 1.3x (Standard) | 1.35x (Complex) | 1.5x (Prototype) | 2.0x (Experimental)
  • Time Logic: Pre-machining is ONE-TIME per part. Setup is ONE-TIME per setup. Machining & Quality multiply by quantity.
  • Validation: Required fields are marked with * and validated automatically.
  • Print Report: Generates a clean, professional PDF without interface elements.
